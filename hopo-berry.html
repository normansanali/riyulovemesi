The Hopo Berry (Japanese: ポフのみ Pofu Fruit) is a type of Berry introduced in Pokémon Legends: Arceus. Its effect is similar to that of the Leppa Berry.

Effect

When used from the satchel on a Pokémon, it restores the PP of one of its moves by 10. When thrown from the satchel onto a wild Pokémon, its reactions will be dulled.
