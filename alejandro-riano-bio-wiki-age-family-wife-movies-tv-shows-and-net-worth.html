Alejandro Riaño Career

Riaño has worked as an actor in some film productions such as Todos para uno by Harold Trompetero (2014), Malcriados by Felipe Martínez Amador (2016) and Empeliculados by Diego Fernando Bustamante (2017). Since the early 2000s, he has worked as a theater actor, joining the cast of the Casa del Teatro Nacional and performing both comedic and dramatic in his native country and internationally.

His career as a comedian began in his teens, later collaborating with humorist Andrés López, whom Riaño considers one of his main influences. He has created several stand-up comedy shows, including Truth, lie or gossip, Each child with his ballot, Stupid things to laugh at, and Stand-up special. This latest production was included in the repertoire of the Netflix streaming company in 2018. He has also been a television presenter and host in programs such as La Sopa Colombia, Los comediantes de la Noche andOur week, our TV.
