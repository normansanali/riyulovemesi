Re: Driving tractor across Leech field

The basic line is that it all relies on the architecture, age and components of your system. In most circumstances it does not matter and you may drive over it with no difficulties. If you system is failing it probably has nothing to do with your tractor.

Keeping this in perspective, can you drive a tractor over a septic field?

A couple times spread apart when the ground is dry is acceptable. If the ground is moist, you want to restrict it to just a few of times. That is everything for the leech field. As far as the septic tank, you really don’t want to drive over it with anything more than a garden tractor.
