Johnny McDaid And Courteney Cox Were Engaged At One Point

Following a year of romantic bliss, Johnny McDaid and Courteney Cox found themselves at the pinnacle of a lifetime commitment. Unfortunately, the tides turned on their relationship about a year later, casting a shadow on their future together. Cox would later comment on what the circumstances of the split during a 2016 episode of Running Wild with Bear Grylls.

“We were engaged for over a year, and then we broke up,” she said. “He's from Ireland. And the way he regards love is precious. We have to treat it in a different way. It's more special — you coddle it. So I didn't know how to regard love the way he does. And I definitely made a lot of mistakes that I see — whether it's codependency or people-pleasing. Will Courteney Cox And Johnny McDaid Ever Get Married?

Having defied the odds by rekindling their romance, fans expected that Courteney Cox and Johnny McDaid would proceed with their wedding plans. Unfortunately, as the years trod on, it became evident that the twosome was not particularly interested in walking down the aisle. "He's not my fiancé,” Cox said of her relationship with McDaid during her 2019 appearance on The Ellen DeGeneres Show. “We were engaged to be married, but now we're just together. We broke off our engagement, and he moved to England, then we got back together, and it's actually better than it was before."

The acclaimed actress seemed even more nonchalant about tying the knot in her 2022 interview with People, responding, "I don't know. I'm definitely not opposed to it. I just don't think about it," when asked if she plans to tie the knot any time soon. Similarly, McDaid is not keen on rushing down the aisle, despite being completely smitten with Courteney. "I just want to be with this woman for the rest of my life,” he told People in 2015. “That is all that matters. The ceremonies are academic."

Though they've yet to walk down the aisle, Johnny and Courteney’s devotion to each other remains unwavering. “I don’t know if you can put a label on what makes us work,” McDaid said of his relationship with Courteney in a 2018 interview with People, “but I know that I’ve never loved like I love this woman, so if that’s enough, then that’s enough.”

Despite defying convention by relegating matrimony, Courteney is still intent on building a family with McDaid. "I would love to have a baby now," she told NewBeauty magazine in 2017. "I know it's crazy, but I would. … I could carry someone else's egg. I may be one of the older people doing it, but I would love to, with Johnny that is."
