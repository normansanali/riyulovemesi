Selling Sunset’s spinoff, Selling Tampa was all set to premiere on Dec 15, 2021, and the “all-Black, all-women” cast looked more promising than ever. But among them, Alexis Williams, a realtor from Tampa, Florida, had a different vibe about her.

This Alexis Williams Wiki explores everything you need to know about this Selling Tampa participant.

Alexis Williams On Selling Tampa

Netflix’s Selling Tampa followed the ongoings at Allure Realty. “Sharelle has big plans for her brokerage and won’t let anyone or anything get in the way of making her dreams a reality,” Netflix’s description read.

As the leader, Sharelle Rosado instructed the group of 8 who were “equally as fun as they (were) fiercely ambitious.”

The other participants joining Alexis in the inaugural season were — Anne-Sophie Petit, Colony Reeves, Juawana Willaims, Karla Giorgio, Rena Frazier, and Tennille Moore.

Most of them were a realtor by occupation, while Rena Frazier is both an agent and a lawyer. Meanwhile,  Tennille Moore also described herself as a “Certified Credit Repair Specialist.”

Excited to be on the show, Alexis took it to her IG on Nov 23, 2021, to write, “Thanks to the cast of Selling Sunset for the fierce welcome to the Netflix family!” She added, “That’s right, I’m part of the new reality series, Selling Tampa, and I can’t wait for you to see what’s in store on Dec 15th only on Netflix.”

Also, she followed the post with another promotional post, featuring all 8 ladies. “Look at me and my gorgeous colleagues dominating the real estate market!”, she captioned.

For those who are new to the show, Allure Realty is a real estate company, nominated for the “Best Real Estate Company” honor during Best of the Bay 2021. Besides the company’s achievement, their realtors have celebrated their wins as well. Just in Oct 2020, cast member Anne-Sophie Petit made it to the list of the top 5% of realtors nationally.

Alexis Williams Age

Alexis Williams was born on July 16, 1991. That made her 30 years of age when she first appeared on Selling Tampa in 2021.

Corresponding to her birthday, Alexis is of the Cancer zodiac.

How Much Is Alexis Williams Net Worth?

Alexis Williams garnered a net worth of above $400 thousand by 2021.

A full-time real estate agent, Alexis joined the industry in the spring of 2017. Since then, she has sold numerous homes around Auburndale, Fl, and has gained specialization in “Communication, Local Expertise, and Professionalism.”

Last time we checked, she had listed a few houses that ranged anywhere from $250 thousand to $1 million.

Alexis shared that her “resume is unique” and was excited to use the “education and tools (she) gleaned.” And maybe this was what separated Alexis from the rest. Her compassion, negotiation skills, and unique marketing strategies were appreciated by many.

Her bio on Allure Realty read, “No two deals are alike and no two properties are alike. Every Buyer and Seller are different, have different wants, needs and financial goals,” 

As for her education, she is a Sante Fe Community College graduate.

Does Alexis Williams Have A Partner?

Yes, Alexis Williams had a partner before she joined Selling Tampa. His name is Mike Gillislee. Yes, the former American football running back. They were reportedly together at least since 2009. However, Mike only appeared on Alexis’s socials after 2017.

According to Alexis, Mike is the best partner she could ever wish for. “I’m so lucky to have you. We inspire each other daily & I’m so thankful we get to share these memories,” Alexis wrote on Mike’s 29th birthday (i.e. on November 2, 2019).

The couple gave birth to a son named Jordan back on August 30, 2015. He has made quite a few appearances over his parent’s IG. And from the looks of it, Mike was already planning to make Jordan follow in his footsteps.

But Mike is a great dad. Alexis shared that he always went “above and beyond every day” for their family. “I’m so grateful to be sharing life, love, and parenthood with a wonderful man like (Mike),” she added.

Little info about Alexis’s partner, Mike Gillislee is a 5’11 football player from Deland, FL, USA, who went on to play for New Orleans Saints in 2018. Before that, he was signed with New England Patriots, Buffalo Bills, Arizona Cardinals, and Miami Dolphins.

He is a year older than Alexis.
