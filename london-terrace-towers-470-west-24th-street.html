London Terrace Towers, 470 West 24th Street, Unit 16H - 1 Bed Apt for Sale for $999,000

September 04, 2024

An elegant, classic prewar one-bedroom home with stunning city views located in London Terrace, situated within the heart of West Chelsea, just steps from the Highline. As you enter the residence through the spacious foyer, you’ll appreciate the walk-in coat closet for your convenience. This beautiful home is in excellent condition and features a massive, extra-wide living/dining room with high ceilings and crown molding, perfect for entertaining guests.

The renovated kitchen features stainless steel appliances, stone counters, and a glass mosaic backsplash. New walnut-stained white oak floors, beautiful molding, gorgeous light fixtures, and hardware throughout add to the apartment’s timeless charm. The king-sized bedroom boasts stunning arched casement windows, and there is a hallway walk-in closet for additional storage.

The spacious bathroom embraces its prewar roots with subway-tiled walls, a tremendous pedestal sink, an original cast-iron tub, and two medicine cabinets, one with a full-length mirror. The apartment also boasts skim-coated walls and 9’ ceilings. London Terrace Towers also offers a range of amenities, including a 1/2 Olympic size indoor heated pool, steam rooms, showers, and saunas in the men’s and women’s private locker rooms. For those looking to stay in shape, there’s a state-of-the-art resident-only health facility available with memberships.

Plus, bike rooms and storage lockers are available for a nominal monthly fee. The building features 24-hour lobby attendants, a live-in Superintendent, and full-time porters. Additionally, pets are welcome on a case-by-case basis, and unlimited subleasing is allowed after the first year of ownership.

With Hudson River Park, Hudson Yards, Chelsea Piers, Chelsea Market, and some of New York City’s finest restaurants and hottest nightspots practically at your doorstep, this unbelievable location offers the perfect blend of lifestyle and convenience. Please note that there is a 2% flip tax, which is split equally between the buyer and seller.
