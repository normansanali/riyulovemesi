Starfield Release Date &amp; Time: Countdown To Launch

September 18, 2024
blog

We count down to the exact date and time of Starfield's speculated release date as Bethesda gets ready for launch.

First Published August 14, 2023, 06:14

Bethesda's massive, upcoming Sci-Fi RPG, Starfield, is set to feature over 1,000 explorable planets, an intriguing story, and all the space exploration gamers desire. It was supposed to be released on 11 November 2022, but following a delay, an exact new launch date has not been revealed.

This article explores when we can expect Starfield to launch, including the date, time, and a countdown clock to the speculated release date, so let's get ready for launch!

Update on 14 August 2023: Starfield's release date has officially been revealed! Check out our countdown timer so you can stare at the days and hours ticking away.

Starfield Release Date Confirmed!

UPDATE: Starfield's release date is 6 September 2023. The original article follows.

Via the Bethesda support website, the publisher confirmed that "Starfield arrives exclusively on Xbox Series X|S and PC in the first half of 2023." That's still a pretty big release date window for Starfield.

However, fans think they've narrowed it down to an exact date for when they can join the Constellation, aka the "last group of space explorers." Some fans are exploring what the exact date would be, and one date really does make a lot of sense: 12 April 2023.

Bethesda likes meaningful release dates. For example, they wanted to release Starfield on 11/11/22. Fallout 4 was released on 10 November 2015, the 240th anniversary of the Marine Corps, one day before Veteran's Day.

So why the 12 April 2023 release date? Well, that's International Day of Human Space Flight. For those who don't know, that's the anniversary of the first time humans went to space, back in 1961! It would make a whole lot of sense for the game to release on the same day.

As for the time Starfield would release, this remains much more unclear. We do suspect that Starfield will release globally at the same time, possibly at midnight on 12 April 2023. Alternatively, as Bethesda did with Fallout 4, it could release one day before the International Day of Human Space Flight, on 11 April 2023.
