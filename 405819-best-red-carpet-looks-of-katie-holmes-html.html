Best Red Carpet Looks of Katie Holmes Through the Years

September 10, 2024
blog

During the 2023 Cannes Film Festival, Katie Holmes attended Kering's Women in Motion Talk event in Cannes, France, and her stylish ensemble was certainly a highlight. Opting for a casual-cool look, the actress wore a lime green T-shirt layered with a breezy yellow button-up, paired with matching palazzo pants.

The baggy, wide-legged pants were accentuated by a sleek belt line, creating a striking silhouette. Holmes traded the typical sky-high Cannes heels for Le Monde Beryl's blue satin knotted kitten heel mules, making a fashion statement with their minimal 35mm heel.

Her look was expertly curated by stylist Brie Welch, with hair styled by Jennifer Yepez and makeup by Hung Vanngo. Katie Holmes' style has inspired for years, so let's take a closer look at her outfits for different events over the decades.

1995

Exuding a romantic aura, Katie Holmes graced the 47th Annual Emmy Awards in a dreamy white maxi dress posing with James Van Der Beek for the photo. The attire boasted a deep square neckline and was adorned with intricate stem-and-leaves embroidery throughout.

Katie Holmes and James Van Der Beek at the 47th Annual Emmy Awards | Source: Getty Images

Complementing her outfit, Holmes carried a small purse designed in the form of a flower ball and donned white striped heels. Her look was further enhanced with dazzling crystal accessories. As for her hair, it was elegantly styled in romantic curls.

2005

Back in June 2005, Katie Holmes and Tom Cruise were the epitome of a sizzling Hollywood couple, creating a stir as they arrived on a motorcycle for the L.A. premiere of Cruise's film, "War of the Worlds."

Katie Holmes and Tom Cruise at the "War of the Worlds" Los Angeles premiere | Source: Getty Images

Their matching style was hard to ignore, both opting for fashionable leather jackets, stylish sunglasses, and boot-cut jeans. Holmes stole the spotlight in a burgundy leather jacket, dark sunglasses, and form-fitting jeans that sat low on her hips, effortlessly matching Cruise's energy.

2007

At the 2007 Vanity Fair Oscar Party, Katie Holmes was also seen with Tom Cruise. Holmes wowed the crowd in an elegant strapless beige organza dress.

Tom Cruise and Katie Holmes during the 2007 Vanity Fair Oscar Party | Source: Getty Images

The actress accessorized her outfit with a petite golden clutch and a matching cape that perfectly accentuated her ensemble. To complete her glamorous look, Holmes wore her hair up in a sleek bun, held in place by an exquisite hair claw.

2010

At the 68th Annual Golden Globe Nomination announcement held on December 14, 2010, in Beverly Hills, California, Katie Holmes made a chic appearance. The actress was captivating in a black-striped ivory dress with a pleated knee-length skirt.

Katie Holmes at the 68th Annual Golden Globe Awards nominations | Source: Getty Images

Adding a touch of sophistication, she opted for nude pointed-toe heels. Holmes added a playful touch with her loose, voluminous hair.

2012

At the 2012 Vanity Fair Oscars party, Katie Holmes made her last appearance with Tom Cruise, who is the father of her daughter Suri Cruise. Holmes dazzled in a strapless navy blue maxi dress adorned with sequins, beadwork, and embroidery at the top.

Tom Cruise and Katie Holmes at the 2012 Vanity Fair Oscar Party | Source: Getty Images

The actress styled her hair in a high ponytail with straight bangs. Adding a bold accent to her outfit, Holmes wore a substantial golden chain around her neck.

2013

In 2013, Katie Holmes graced the Met Gala, themed "PUNK: Chaos To Couture," in an exceptional outfit. She turned heads in a backless white Calvin Klein gown that showcased her fabulous figure.

Katie Holmes at the Costume Institute Gala for the 'PUNK: Chaos to Couture' exhibition | Source: Getty Images

The long strapless gown featured a round neck detail and an extended train, making a significant impression. Her sophisticated updo hairstyle completed the captivating ensemble.

2014

At the 2014 Met Gala, Katie Holmes dazzled, resembling a Disney princess in a striking goldenrod-yellow Marchesa ball gown. The off-the-shoulder number boasted layers and delicate embroidery across the front, with the fabric creating a unique crinkling effect around the thighs before falling into a smooth full skirt.

Katie Holmes at the "Charles James: Beyond Fashion" Costume Institute Gala | Source: Getty Images

Staying true to her recent style preferences, the actress chose to wear her hair down, showcasing the beachy waves she's been favoring.

2017

At the American Ballet Theater's spring gala at Lincoln Center on May 22, 2017, Katie Holmes was a vision in a Zac Posen creation. The actress looked stunning in a strapless scarlet gown, adorned with intricate floral detailing.

Katie Holmes at the American Ballet Theatre Spring 2017 Gala | Source: Getty Images

The dress was characterized by a voluminous skirt and a loose-fitting bodice, further accentuating its floral embellishments, embodying an elegant blend of simplicity and sophistication.

2019

Katie Holmes graced the Met Gala in a custom-made Zac Posen gown that encapsulated the night's camp theme perfectly. The mermaid-style dress, made from 300 yards of hand-sewn, multi-layered purple tulle, was a collaborative effort with her close friend, Zac Posen.

Katie Holmes at The Metropolitan Museum of Art's 2019 Costume Institute Benefit "Camp: Notes On Fashion" | Source: Getty Images

The standout feature was a stunning 3D-printed palm leaves collar, produced by Protolabs, which took over 56 hours to create. This high-tech accessory beautifully contrasted with the traditional craftsmanship of the gown, underlining Holmes' unique fashion statement.

2022

At the 2022 CFDA Awards in New York City, Katie Holmes channeled a modern flapper aesthetic in a striking Simkhai ensemble. Her dress showcased a long, tank-like silhouette with a swishy crystal fringe at the hemline, an all-over cage finish, and a scoop neckline that added a contemporary twist to the classic 1920s style.

Katie Holmes at the 2022 CFDA Fashion Awards at Casa Cipriani | Source: Getty Images

Holmes topped off the look with vampy, dark red lips, a slicked-back bun, and sparkling heels.
