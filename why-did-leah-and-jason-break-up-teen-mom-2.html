'Teen Mom 2' Star Leah Messer Speaks Out After Breakup With Boyfriend Jason Jordan

September 18, 2024
blog

Here's what you need to know about 'Teen Mom 2' star Leah Messer and ex-boyfriend Jason Jordan's break up.

It’s over! Leah Messer and boyfriend Jason Jordan have ended their relationship. Us Weekly reported the breakup news after the two reconciled in November 2018. 

Though the Teen Mom 2 star waited to officially confirm her split weeks later, fans started questioning her relationship status. 

The 26-year-old posted a cryptic message on her Instagram story on March 13, writing, "When someone shows you who they are believe them. Facts."

The following day, Leah decided to vent via the ‘Gram and posted the caption, "Everyone comes into our lives for a reason, a season or a lifetime. I’m thankful for everyone I’ve journeyed with and continue to learn and grow." Preach, girl! 

However, as fans continued to question if her posts were about her “reported ex,” Leah finally set the record on her relationship straight. She tweeted: “Just to be very clear, Nothing that I post is in reference to my recent break up or Jason! I wish him the best!!!!” Ouch.

So, why did Leah and Jason break up?

This is certainly not the first time the two decided to end their relationship. Before the holidays, the MTV starlet said goodbye to her 40-year-old beau.

“It just wasn’t working,” a source told Us at the time. “They’re just not a good fit.” 

The reality star also told HollywoodLife.com that her ex-beau was not in the right mindset to be in a committed relationship, saying, "He wasn’t in a great place in his life, and it is what it is. It’s fine."

Leah didn’t stay single for long and the two decided to give love another shot. 

Why Did Leah and Jason get back together? 

While the pair reconciled in late November, they reportedly ended their relationship at the beginning of of last month. The former couple initially met through Jordan’s cousin and were reportedly “getting serious” last July.  

“They share the same faith and have gone to church together,” a source told Us Weekly at the time. And their 14-year age difference was seemingly not a negative factor in their relationship, the source cited, claiming: “Even though he’s older, they have a lot of common. They love hiking, cooking and are both very family oriented. He’s very attentive.” 

While the couple decided to part ways, we can’t rule out a possible reunion. In Teen Mom world, you certainly never know what ex will pop back up on our TV screens.  

For now, with her ex in her rearview mirror, Leah seems to be finding comfort with her daughters —  Aliannah and Aleeah, 9, and Adalynn, 6. She recently posted a gorgeous Instagram pic of herself with the three girls, which she captioned, "They will forever be my reasoning for a successful and abundantly joyful simple LIFE! Everything I do, I do it for them."
