About

Rob Kardashian is best known for starring on the E! reality TV show Keeping Up With the Kardashians (KUWTK) alongside his famous family. He is the youngest child of the late Robert Kardashian and media mogul Kris Jenner, who also shared daughters Kourtney, Kim and Khloé Kardashian. Kendall and Kylie Jenner, Rob’s half-sisters, also starred on KUWTK.

Rob competed on season 13 of Dancing with the Stars in 2011, finishing in second place with his professional partner Cheryl Burke. His designer sock line, Arthur George, was launched in 2012.

Rob dated Cheetah Girls star adrienne Bailon from 2007 to 2009, splitting due to Rob’s alleged infidelity. After briefly dating singer Rita Ora in 2012, Rob moved on with Blac Chyna in January 2016. The couple got engaged three months later. Their reality TV show, Rob & Chyna, premiered in September 2016. Chyna gave birth to their daughter, Dream, two months later. (Rob and Chyna called it quits in 2017.)

KUWTK documented Rob’s struggles over the years, but he eventually moved away from the spotlight and chose not to be featured on The Kardashians when the family moved to Hulu in 2021.
