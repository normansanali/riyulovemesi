Kojo Vypa Releases Monster On Earth Freestyle 2

July 31, 2024
blog

Young versatile Ghanaian rapper and hip hop recording artiste, Kojo Vypa finally delivers the second episode of ‘Monster On Earth Freestyle’.

The new output follows his previous and trending single; “Wona“. ‘Monster On Earth Freestyle 2’ is a sequel to the first freestyle which was released few months ago. The project received a lot of positive reviews and comments.

The new release, ‘MOEF 2’ is accompanied by a well curated video jointly directed, shot and edited by Paade Dadas and Yung Guru. It was filmed on location at Cape Coast, Elmina and Komenda.

Kojo Vypa, real name Desmond Prince Owusu is an outstanding artiste who hails from the Central Region of Ghana. He has been constantly tagged as one of the very talented acts to watch out for in the coming years within the Ghanaian music circles.

Kojo’s hard work, lyrical dexterity and delivery are among the numerous features that can’t be ignored. He’s well known for his song ‘Convoy’ with Ayesem and also appeared on Article Wan’s tune, ‘Etso’ where he served a dope verse to spice up the track.
