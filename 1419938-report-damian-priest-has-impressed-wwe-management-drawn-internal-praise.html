Report: Damian Priest Has Impressed WWE Management, Drawn Internal Praise

September 29, 2024
blog

Damian Priest continues to impress people backstage, according to a new report.

Priest has been featured on WWE RAW in recent months. Alongside his stablemates in The Judgment Day, he is often presented as one of the brand’s top stars. Priest won the Money in the Bank briefcase at WWE Money in the Bank, and he currently holds the Undisputed WWE Tag Team Championship with Finn Balor.

Mike Johnson of PWInsider reports that there has been a lot of praise internally for Priest. Johnson wrote that, in recent months, Priest has impressed management with how hard and physical he has worked. Priest has also drawn praise for gutting through at times when he has been banged up.

Priest and Balor defeated The New Day (Xavier Woods and Kofi Kingston) on the November 6 episode of WWE RAW. The duo will defend their titles against Cody Rhodes and Jey Uso on the November 13 episode.
