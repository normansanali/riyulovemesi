Sachin Tyagi is one of the popular television actors who work in a variety of Hindi TV serials and commercials. His skills are not just limited to acting, yet he is a great singer as well and has been constantly enthralling his fans with his incredible talent. He has acted in many Indian TV serials but mainly known for the role of Swayam in the serial “Kahin Toh Hoga” which had run from 2003 to 2007 on Star Plus. Sachin Tyagi is basically from Meerut, India where he grew up and studied and after completing his studies, started a career.

Career

In 2003, Sachin Tyagi took the start of his career with a small screen and appeared in a debut serial “Kahin To Hoga” which was aired on Star Plus and it became the reason for his constant success and much recognition. Subsequently, he then appeared in various Indian TV serials and commercials for which he got much attention. Before joining the industry, he took the start of his career as a theater artist and acted in a variety of plays. After making debut with “Kahin Toh Hoga” (2003-2007), he then played a small role in the most-watched drama “Kyunki Saas Bhi Kabhi Bahu Thi” which was aired on Star Plus. Later, he made an appearance in the serial “Saarthi” and also acted in “Sara Aakash” for which he was much praised by everyone. Consequently, he also won many awards and honors. Thereafter, he then appeared in “Solah Singhaar” in which he appeared as Vedant Sehgal and it was aired on Zee TV. Additionally, he then made an appearance in “Tumhari Disha”. However, he has established himself a great actor in the TV industry and has played various roles in most popular projects.

Personal Life

In 2014, Sachin Tyagi got married to “Rakshanda Khan” who is also a popular TV actress, and the couple is blessed with a daughter who was born in the same year.
