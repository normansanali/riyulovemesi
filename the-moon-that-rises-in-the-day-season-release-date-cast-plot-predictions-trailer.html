“The Moon that Rises in the Day” is an upcoming South Korean Netflix Original romantic thriller that defies conventions. Directed by Pyo Min Soo, this series is a captivating live-action adaptation of the popular webtoon of the same name. Centered around a man whose time has halted and a woman who ebbs through life like a river, this story delves into their complex relationship. With a blend of mystery and suspense, the series unravels their intertwined destinies, forcing them to confront their deepest fears. Here is everything we know about the upcoming series.

The Moon that Rises in the Day Release Date: When Will it Air?

Netflix or the South Korean cable network E.N.A. have not officially confirmed the release date for The Moon that Rises in the Day. Filming commenced in South Korea around late April 2023 and is projected to continue until mid-August 2023. The series is anticipated to have a late weekly release in 2023, and the episodes may extend into early 2024.

The Moon that Rises in the Day Plot: What Will you Get to See in the Series?

The Moon that Rises in the Day” is not just a love story; it is a captivating romance thriller that delves into the lives of a man trapped in timeless existence and a woman whose life flows like a river. It explores themes of fate, redemption, and the enduring power of love in the face of adversity. The story goes beyond a simple love story, delving into a web of past and present strife. As the man’s time stands still, he encounters the mysterious woman, sparking a desire for connection.

However, their relationship is entangled in a sinister pattern of mystery and danger. With a blend of suspense and passion, the story unravels their intertwined destinies, revealing shocking revelations and forcing them to confront their deepest fears. It challenges the boundaries of time and tests the limits of human resilience.

The Moon that Rises in the Day Cast: Who Will Appear in the Show?

The lead role of Kang Young Hwa will be portrayed by Pyo Ye Jin, known for her role in the Netflix Original series Lovestruck in the City. Her other K-dramas, such as V.I.P., What’s Wrong With Secretary Kim, and Taxi Driver, can also be found on Netflix. Kim Young Dae will lead Do Ha, marking his first appearance in a Netflix series. Ohn Joo Won takes on the lead role of Han Min Oh, making his official Netflix debut with The Moon that Rises in the Day. He is recognized for performing in Beautiful Gong Shim and The Village: Achiara’s Secret. Supporting the main cast are Bae Eun Young (Racket Boys), Jung Heon (No Matter What), Jung Woong In (Prison Playbook), Jung Shin Hye (I Love You, Loser), and Lee Joon Hyuk (100 Days My Prince).
