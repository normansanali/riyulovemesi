Agnes Bruckner is an American actress. She started her Hollywood career in the late 1990s. Her debut was as Bridget Forrester in the soap opera The Bold and the Beautiful. In the early 2000s, she had notable roles in the films The Glass House, Blue Car, Murder by Numbers, Stateside, Haven, and Venom. She also appeared as Linda in five episodes of 24. She later appeared as Heather, a recurring character in Private Practice. In the late 2000s, she had roles in the movies Blood & Chocolate and Kill Theory. In the 2010s, she starred in the films Breaking the Girls, The Pact, The Baytown Outlaws, and Wrong Cops. She had also portrayed the title role in the TV movie The Anna Nicole Story. Her most recent work on television was as Deputy Nikki Banks in The Returned.
