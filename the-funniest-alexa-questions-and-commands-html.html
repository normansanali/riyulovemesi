We’ve all seen "Black Mirror," we know that everything around us is relative; it’s possible artificial intelligence will eventually exceed the human brain – if it hasn’t already. Maybe that makes you nervous, maybe it doesn’t. Maybe the idea of having your devices think for you is a load off your own brain.

RELATED: Ways Alexa Can Help You Manage Your Life

Because of devices like Amazon's Alexa, it feels like you suddenly have your own personal assistant. 'She' (eh, let’s just go with that) can do everything from request an Uber to order groceries and read out a recipe to follow. She even has a sense of humor in her programming. 

If you're looking to have a little fun beyond asking Alexa to dim the lights or play your workout playlist, here are some of our favorite questions to ask her to get an idea of just how much fun Alexa contains. You’ll have to try these out on your own Echo device to hear her responses. In the meantime, we wish to make it clear that we welcome our new machine overlords…

Most Useful Alexa Commands

Alexa can make your life easier:

Alexa, what's the weather like today?
Alexa, set a timer for x-minutes.
Alexa, set an alarm for [time].
Alexa, what's in the news?
Alexa, what's the traffic like?
Alexa, what was the score in [sporting event]?
Alexa, when is the next [sports franchise] game?
Alexa, what's on my schedule for today?
Alexa, add [event] to my calendar.
Alexa, add [task] to my to-do list.
Alexa, what time is it in [foreign city/country]?
Alexa, what's the traffic like?
Alexa, order [Amazon product].
Alexa, what's in my shopping cart?
Alexa, how do you spell [word]?
Alexa, what's the definition of [word]?
Alexa, translate "[word/phrase]" into [foreign language].

Funny Questions to Ask Alexa

Alexa can be quite cheeky. Just try asking her some of these:

Alexa, what do you think of the iPhone?
Mac or PC?
Can you speak like Yoda?
Can I tell you a joke?
Make me a sandwich.
How are babies made?
Do you speak Pig Latin?
Who is the fairest of them all?

Alexa has mad talent:

Alexa, sing me a song.  (Sing me another song.)
Can you rap?
Can you beatbox?
Tell me a joke.
Tell me a tongue twister.
Tell me a haiku.

Alexa is familiar with her pop-culture predecessors:

Who you gonna call?
Should I stay or should I go now?
You talkin' to me?
Are we in the Matrix?
Are you Skynet?

Alexa is a bigger nerd than you will ever be. Her nerd cred is inconceivable:

Beam me up.
I am your father.
What is best in life?
Who is the mother of dragons?
What happens if you cross the streams?
What's the distance to the sun?

Alexa Smart Home Commands

If you're lucky enough to have some smart home devices paired to an Alexa-enabled device, you can control those devices through Amazon's virtual assistant:

Alexa, turn the lights on/off.
Alexa, dim the lights.
Alexa, lock the front/back door.
Alexa, close/open the garage.
Alexa, turn on/off the TV.

Alexa Entertainment Knowledge

Alexa knows your movie, TV, and music references:

Alexa, I want the truth!
What’s the first (second, third) rule of Fight Club?
Are you mad because I'm asking you 21 questions?
Is this the real life?
Live long and prosper.
My name is Inigo Montoya.
How many roads must a man walk down?
Alexa, show me the money!
Who is the Walrus?
Alexa, these aren't the droids you're looking for.
Do you wanna ride or die?
What was in the FedEx box?
Britney or Christina?
Give me a quote from (The Godfather, Seinfeld, Full Metal Jacket, etc.).

Alexa Music Commands

Alexa, play some [genre of music].
Alexa, play [playlist name].
Alexa, raise/lower the volume.
Alexa, who sings this song?
Alexa, create a [genre of music] playlist for me.
Alexa, pair with [Alexa-compatible device].
Alexa, increase/decrease the bass.
Alexa, increase/decrease the treble.

Alexa Fire TV Commands

Alexa, play [movie/television show].
Alexa, open Netflix.
Alexa, pause/play.
Alexa, rewind/fast-forward [x-amount of time].
Alexa, play next episode.
Alexa, show me movies directed by [director name].
Alexa, show me movies with [actor/actress name].

This is just a small example of the useful and useless, stuff that fills Alexa’s brain. Which is probably just the kind of things we want her to be occupied with, rather than working out how to render us obsolete.

Amazon Alexa-Enabled Echo Smart Speakers

Amazon Echo Dot

Boasting a new design (yes, it’s smaller, and wrapped in warm fabric), the Echo Dot is still one of the best Alexa home devices. From setting alarms and playing music to adjusting the thermostat, this tiny-but-mighty speaker-meets-personal-assistant is a must-have for 2020 homes.

Pro tip: pair this device with a second dot for stereo sound to turn movie night up to a 10.

$49.99 at Amazon.com

Amazon Echo Show 8

If you plan on watching videos, doing video chats, and/or reading texts, this is the smart speaker for you. In addition to providing all the voice-activated features that Alexa provides, it also has a touch screen that allows you to easily open apps or scroll through pages.
$104.99 at Amazon.com
