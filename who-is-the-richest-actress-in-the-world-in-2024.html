Who is the Richest Actress in the World in 2024?

August 30, 2024
blog

In 2024, the world of film and television is home to some of the wealthiest and most successful actresses. From classic American sitcoms to Marvel blockbusters, these talented women have amassed significant fortunes through their acting careers.

Jami Gertz 

Jami Gertz takes the coveted title of the richest actress in the world in 2024 with an astounding net worth of $3.2 billion. She is renowned for her roles in films such as "The Lost Boys" and "Less Than Zero."

Notably, she is also the owner of the Atlanta Hawks NBA team and has appeared in various TV shows.

Mary-Kate and Ashley Olsen

The Olsen twins, Mary-Kate and Ashley, follow closely with a combined net worth of $500 million. These American actors gained fame from a very young age, starting their careers at just six months old.

Beyond acting, they have ventured into the world of fashion design, achieving remarkable success in the industry.

Reese Witherspoon 

Reese Witherspoon, a two-time Golden Globe Award winner, has established herself as the highest-paid actress in the world with a net worth of $420 million.

Not only is she an accomplished actress, but she also delves into production and owns the clothing line Draper James and the production company Hello Sunshine.

Victoria Principal 

Victoria Principal, with a net worth of $400 million, has transitioned from being a Hollywood actress to a producer, business owner, and writer. Her foray into the skincare industry with Principal Secret is a testament to her diverse entrepreneurial ventures.

Jessica Alba

Jessica Alba, who began her acting and business careers at the age of 13, has amassed a net worth of $390 million.

Notably, she founded The Honest Company, a business distributing baby and household goods, further solidifying her status as a successful businesswoman.

Jennifer Aniston

Jennifer Aniston, with a net worth of $320 million, has received numerous accolades throughout her career, including a Primetime Emmy Award and two Screen Actors Guild Awards.

Hailing from California, she rose to prominence in the 1990s and has since become one of the highest-paid actresses in the world.

Julia Roberts

Julia Roberts, known for her roles in films such as "Mystic Pizza" and "Steel Magnolias," was the first actress to receive a $20 million salary for a film.

With a net worth of $255 million, she has showcased her talent across various genres, including romantic comedies, dramas, thrillers, and action movies.

Sandra Bullock

Sandra Bullock, with a net worth of $250 million, started her career in 1987 and has since become a renowned producer and actress.

Notably, she was ranked as the most influential person in 2010 by Time magazine and was also awarded People magazine's title of "Most Beautiful Woman" in 2015.

Julia Louis-Dreyfus

Julia Louis-Dreyfus has excelled in comedy, producing, and acting, winning numerous awards throughout her career.

With a net worth of $250 million, she has been named Time's most important person and is celebrated for her outstanding performance in the comedic television series "Saturday Night."

Jane Fonda 

Jane Fonda, with a net worth of $200 million, is not only known for her acting career but also for her activism.

She has received several prestigious honors, including the Academy Award and Primetime Emmy Award, while using her platform to advocate for women's rights.

 

These actresses have not only achieved remarkable success in their acting careers but have also ventured into various entrepreneurial endeavors, solidifying their positions as the richest actresses in the world in 2024.

It's worth noting that the net worth information presented is based on the most recent available data as of 2024. The actresses' diverse achievements, from acting to entrepreneurship, continue to inspire and set new benchmarks in the world of entertainment and beyond.
