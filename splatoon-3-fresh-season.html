Splatoon 3 Fresh Season Launching Confirmed by Nintendo

July 08, 2024
blog

Nintendo has announced the launch date and other specifics for the upcoming Fresh Season 2023 of Splatoon 3. Beginning on March 1, 2023, you’ll have access to new gear and the return of a fan-favorite special weapon from a previous game.

The previous season, Drizzle Season ran from September 2022 to November 2022. At present, we are in Chill Season. A new lineup of poses, Tableturf Battle cards, titles and equipment is released every season and this upcoming season is covered with a plethora of discoveries and it is officially confirmed by the Tweet we have provided below.

What New You Will See in Splatoon 3 Fresh Season?

In today’s image dump, Nintendo gave fans a sneak peek at some of the new gear that will be available as part of the new catalog rewards though details on those items have yet to be disclosed. A hat, a hoodie and a pair of headphones all with a camouflage pattern are included.

The Krak-On Splat Roller and the.96 Gal Deco updated versions of classic roller and blaster designs are just two of the new weapons available this Fresh Season. However, the Kraken Royale is back and both of them have it.
