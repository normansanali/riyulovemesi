Hannah Tointon- Biography

Hannah Tointon is an English actress best known for her roles in ‘The Inbetweeners’ and ‘Hollyoaks.’ She was born and reared in Essex and joined an acting club when she was seven years old because her sister was in the same group. She gradually began to embrace the world of acting and theater. She never went to drama school and claims to have learned everything she knows about acting through watching her co-actors on stage.

Tointon made her acting debut in 2003, playing a minor role in the TV series ‘Star,’ and subsequently a major role in the children’s series ‘Kerching!’ She had cameo appearances in programs such as ‘New Tricks’ and ‘Murder City’ after appearing in a recurrent role in ‘Dream Team.’ In 2007, she received her first major break in her acting career when she was cast as a regular in the long-running soap opera ‘Hollyoaks.’ She made her cinematic debut in 2006 and has since played prominent roles in films such as ‘Walking with the Enemy’ and ‘The Lost Future.’
