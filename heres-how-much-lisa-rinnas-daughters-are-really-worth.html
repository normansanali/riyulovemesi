Here's How Much Lisa Rinna's Daughters Are Really Worth

July 19, 2024
blog

Lisa Rinna has covered plenty of magazines throughout her career as a model, including "Soap Opera Digest," "Cv Lux," and of course, "Playboy," per FamousFix. Therefore, it's no surprise that her daughters have inherited her effortless talent to pose and strut their stuff down the runway. Her eldest child, in particular, Delilah Belle Hamlin, has been rising in the entertainment industry ranks.

As she becomes famous in her own right, Delilah — who was born on June 10, 1998 — has already started making her own cash. Despite being in her early 20s, her net worth is already estimated to be $15 million, according to The Famous Data. Most of the bombshell's money is earned through her modeling career, and per WWD, she has been signed with IMG Models since April 2017.

On Instagram, it's common for Delilah to show off her photoshoot snapshots. While there's no doubt she has a future as a supermodel, Delilah revealed she actually had to hold off from pursuing her dreams because Lisa "didn't let [her] model until [she] was 17."

"She knew the culture and the industry because she's been in it for so long, and she didn't really want me to get in it too early," she told Teen Vogue in August 2016. "She wanted me to mature more and understand things more. It is a difficult culture with body type and body image, and she wanted me to wait until I was older."
