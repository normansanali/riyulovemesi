General Hospital Spoilers: Josh Swickard Posts Adorable Photo with Second Baby GH Co-Stars Offer

September 15, 2024
blog

General Hospital spoilers reveal that Harrison Chase (Josh Swickard) might not be having great luck with romance. However, the man who plays the character is a different story. Actor Josh Swickard and his wife, Lauren Swickard, announced the birth of their second child. Keep reading to get the details, including what they named their newest bundle of joy.

Josh Swickard’s Announcement

GH spoilers reveal that Josh and Lauren announced the arrival of their second child on Instagram. Josh posted a photo that included the newborn baby boy all decked out in blue and white. The actor wrote,

“WELCOME TO THE FAMILY ARTHUR!!!! @laurenswickard brought this little guy into the world on February 26th (Lo I’m truly in awe of you). Thank you for all that you sacrificed to grow our family. Arthur buddy, you are so loved already!!! We pray for courage and strength over you, and that God will lead you all of your days!”

GH Spoilers: Big Happy Family

Josh and Lauren married on December 29, 2019. The couple welcomed a daughter on April 21, 2021. Josh and Lauren named their little girl Savannah Swickard. The couple announced their second pregnancy in October 2022.

Then, in December 2022, Josh and Lauren announced that the baby was a boy. Now, little Arthur Swickard has joined the family and everyone is thrilled for the family.

In fact, Josh and Lauren have worked together on projects before, even after getting married.

Precious Family Photo

General Hospital spoilers reveal that Josh and Lauren posted a photo of themselves happily posing with Arthur and Savannah. Of course, the whole family is beaming and glowing with joy. Both Arthur and Savannah are absolutely precious!

General Hospital Spoilers: Fans & Followers React

In the comments section, fans and followers reacted to Josh and Lauren’s baby news.

Several of Josh’s co-stars expressed their congratulations to the family. Some of these include Finola Hughes (Anna Devane), Marcus Coloma (ex-Nikolas Cassadine), Kathleen Gati (Liesl Obrecht), Laura Wright (Carly Spencer) and Kirsten Storms (Maxie Jones).

A few even commented on how they couldn’t wait to meet Little Arthur.

However, stars from the other three American soaps also chimed in. Eileen Davidson (Ashley Abbott, Y&R) and Kate Mansi (ex-Abigail Deveraux DiMera, DOOL) all extended their congratulations.

CDL would like to congratulate Lauren and Josh Swickard on the arrival of their new baby boy.

Savannah and Arthur are both adorable. Here’s to a lifetime of wonderful memories for the Swickard family.
