Kane Spirals Over The Cherie And Jessey Gossip

The second episode of Bling Empire's second season opens with Kane and Kelly paying Dr. TJ Tsay a visit, so Kane can do some touch ups on his face. After dissolving a tablet of ketamine into his mouth and sucking on some laughing gas, Kelly watches as Kane gets his nose and chin threaded, promoting the growth of collagen to add structure to his look.The two then meet Kevin for dinner where Kevin expresses his concern that Kane might be hurting himself with these procedures. "Tomorrow, you're gonna fall in love with my face again," Kane assures him. Kane Delves Into Cherie's Personal Life

At a get together at Guy Tang's house, Jaime reveals to Kelly the news she received from Leah about Cherie regarding whether Cherie knows that Jessey had a wife and two children when the couple met. The pair then turn to the group to gather their opinions about whether to discuss their findings with Cherie.

As a close friend of Cherie's, real estate agent, Kane, takes it upon himself to "find out the truth," though Kevin worries Kane's aptitude for gossip may create unnecessary drama. "I think they should just kind of let it go," he says.

Yet while Kevin's advice is sound, Kane invites Jaime and Leah to lunch to understand the situation to the fullest. Leah reveals that Cherie was dating an ex named Alex before she met Jessey. Alex introduced Cherie to Jessey and his wife, Crystal, with whom he shares two children. Alex and Cherie called it quits upon finding out that Cherie and Jessey were involved.

Kane takes the shocking information personally, questioning his relationship with Cherie. "This is not the Cherie I knew," Kane says, upset that he had to hear this news about Cherie's personal life from a third party rather than from the horse's mouth. With that, Kane plops the table's bread in his bag and departs, prepared to wallow in food.

Kim Opens Up About Her Relationship Struggles

Excited about the prospect of a relationship with Kim, Kevin pays for a private date with a chef named Jasper whose intent was to offer the couple a gourmet meal in conjunction with teaching them about the art of preparing a fish. Flowers in hand, Kevin arrives at the dinner spot, telling Jasper he's going to be accompanied by a special someone.

At 8:08 PM, Kevin tries calling Kim to no avail. "Kim's always on time, if not early," Kevin insists, calling her once again at 8:25 PM. Finally, at 8:44 PM, Kevin tries Kim for the final time. When received by her voice mailbox once again, Kevin decides to give up.

At Guy's party, Kevin and Kim run into each other for the first time since she stood him up on their date, and though she tried to avoid the conversation, Kevin pulls her aside and asks her for an explanation. "I'm not sure if I wanna go there with you," Kim admits, adding that she doesn't want to lose their friendship were they not to work out. Despite his mild heartbreak, Kevin accepts her apology.

At Kelly's house later in the week, Kim sits with Kelly and her mom, Kimmy, and further divulges that the reason she avoided a date with Kevin was due to past relationship trauma where she felt betrayed by her ex. "When I'm in a relationship," Kim notes through teary eyes, "I give my all, and that's very scary to me." Kimmy assures her that "time heals everything," allowing Kim to breathe a hopeful sigh of relief.

Viewers Question Kim's Intentions

Despite Kim having opened up to Kelly and her mother about past relationship turmoil, fans are still upset that she wasn't mature enough to own up to her feelings in conversation with Kevin. Rather than being real with him, she decided the best course of action was to stand him up, proving that she may not be on the right track toward becoming a successful partner.

Kane's Affinity For Gossip Is Sure To Get Messy

While Kane claims that his want to get to the bottom of things with the Cherie gossip is out of the goodness of his heart and want to maintain a closeness with her as friends. However, it seems the twinkle in his eye that arises upon hearing and spreading rumors will prove to be a saboteur. His sit down with Leah and Jaime seemed to be for no reason other than to further intwine himself in the gossip. If he's going to preserve the friendship he cherishes with Cherie, he'll need to heed Kevin's advice and take a step back from the rumor mill.
