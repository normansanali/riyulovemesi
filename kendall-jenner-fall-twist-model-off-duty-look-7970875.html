Kendall Jenner Just Put a Fall Twist on Her Signature Model-Off-Duty Look

September 08, 2024
blog

With the paparazzi constantly following her every move, Kendall Jenner is never technically "off duty." But when she's not walking the runway or hitting up a fashion party in some far-flung part of the world, she can likely be spotted out on the sidewalk, dressed in her signature uniform of jeans and a T-shirt. 

On Saturday, that was exactly case when Jenner stepped out for lunch at Bar Pitti in New York City — however, with fall just around the corner, she added an autumnal twist to her go-to outfit formula. For the weekend outing, the supermodel went braless underneath a white cropped tee, and paired the top with bootcut dark-wash jeans and an oversized chocolate brown denim jacket to keep warm. She accessorized the ultra-baggy coat (which looked like it could've belonged to her boyfriend Bad Bunny, BTW) with a black leather duffle-style bag and matching fall-favorite loafers. 

Oval-shaped sunglasses, tiny hoops, and a black leather belt with a gold buckle provided the finishing touches to Kendall's low-key look. Her dark brunette hair was worn down and straight with a center part, and she complemented her dewy skin with a swipe of dark pink lipstick. 

Just a day earlier, Kendall delivered a lesson in early fall dressing once again. Wearing the same jeans and loafers, Kendall continued to keep it simple with a white muscle tank top. She carried a similar large leather bag (this time, in a cognac shade) with a cozy gray sweater covered with colorful flowers draped underneath its handles — just in case it got chilly.
