Meet Tee Morant, Ja Morant Father

Tee Morant is Ja Morant’s father who came to cheer his son like any other day for his game vs Minnesota Timberwolves. But little did he know that he would get famous after being seated next to Usher, wearing a matching black t-shirt and aviator sunglasses.

The duo was even shown on the Grizzlies’ “look-alike” cam during a timeout break and the crowd went crazy as the pair shook hands.

Naturally, the clip also blew up over the internet, and fans stated opinionating. “They’re literally twins,” a user wrote. While another tweeted, “Seems like they’re about to become best friends.”

Tee Morant Job

Tee is a barber. After narrowly missing playing in the NBA after his career at Claflin University Panthers, and finding out that his wife was pregnant with Ja, he left basketball to support the family.

However, Tee still has kept his passion for basketball alive, offering coaching classes through his eponymous “skill and agility training” program.

Talking about his basketball career, Tee played alongside Ray Alle at Hillcrest High School, Dalzell, SC, and went on to play college basketball at Claflin University. Later, he then went on to participate in free agency camps and play internationally until his professional aspirations were cut short because of a family burden.

But his dream now continues in his son Ja. He is actively involved in his son’s basketball development.

Tee Morant Wife

Tee Morant’s wife Jamie Morant is also a former basketball player. She used to be a point guard in high school and a softball player in college.
