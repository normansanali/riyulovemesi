Roman Ildonzo Gabriel, Jr. is a retired American football player who was born on August 5, 1940. He was the first Asian-American to start as an NFL quarterback and is widely regarded as one of the top quarterbacks of the late 1960s and early 1970s. He was the son of a Filipino immigrant. He was the second overall pick in the 1962 NFL Draft and spent eleven seasons with the Los Angeles Rams before joining the Philadelphia Eagles for five seasons.
