Why Kelsea Ballerini And Morgan Evans Got Divorced

August 27, 2024
blog

Kelsea Ballerini and Morgan Evans have been one of the cutest celebrity couples in the country music scene since they were married in 2017. The couple had been together since 2016 and fans of the singers loved them together. Ballerini rose to popularity in country music in 2014 with her single “Love Me Like You Mean It.” Since then, she has gone on to release many singles and albums, and is well loved by country music fans. Evans, while not as famous as Ballerini, also works in the music industry.

Unfortunately, Ballerini and Evans announced that they were getting a divorce in 2022. The news came as a shock to fans of the couple, as the singers were always so positive about their relationship. Ballerini even wrote many loving songs about her ex-husband. Let’s get into why Kelsea Ballerini and Morgan Evans are getting a divorce.

8 Who Is Kelsea Ballerini?

Kelsea Ballerini is a country music singer and songwriter. She was first signed to a music label in 2014. Her first single, “Love Me Like You Mean It,” rose to the top of the charts. Since stepping onto the scene, Ballerini has released four studio albums, one of which was comprised of two counterpart albums.

When she signed her first record deal, Ballerini was only nineteen years old. She had been writing music since she was a child and left school to pursue a career in country music. Outside of music, Ballerini is also a poet. She released a collection of poetry titled Feel Your Way Through on November 16, 2021.

7 Who Is Morgan Evans?

Like his ex-wife Kelsea Ballerini, Morgan Evans is a country music singer and songwriter. He released his self-titled debut album in 2014. He is originally from Australia, but relocated to Nashville, Tennessee in 2017. Following the move, Evans signed with a Nashville based record label.

One of Evans’s most successful singles is his 2018 song “Diamonds.” The singer has stated that the song is a love song dedicated to Ballerini, whom he had been with since their first meeting in 2016, which only made fans love the song even more.

6 How Did Kelsea Ballerini And Morgan Evans Meet?

As they are both country music singers, Kelsea Ballerini and Morgan Evans were bound to meet eventually. They first met in 2016 when they were asked to co-host Australia’s Country Music Channel Awards show. At the after part for the event, sparks were definitely flying between the two singers.

“That night be leaned over and asked if he could kiss me like a proper gentleman,” Ballerini said of the night they first met. “And I said yes.” The two quickly began seeing each other romantically. Fans of Ballerini and Evans loved seeing how happy they were together.

5 Kelsea Ballerini Married Morgan Evans

On Christmas Day in 2016, Kelsea Ballerini and Morgan Evans announced their engagement on social media. He proposed in the kitchen while Ballerini was making pancakes. While fans were overjoyed for the couple, they were also a little jealous of the stunning ring.

Almost one year later, on December 2nd, 2017, Ballerini and Evans tied the knot. They had a destination wedding on the beach in Cabo San Lucas, Mexico. They reportedly had a semi-tequila-themed wedding, since the night they first met they drank tequila.

4 Kelsea Ballerini Wrote Songs About Morgan Evans

As both Kelsea Ballerini and Morgan Evans are both songwriters, it is no surprise that their love was an inspiration for a lot of music. On Ballerini’s highly successful album kelsea, which was released in 2020, many songs told the story of their romance. Fans particularly loved Ballerini’s praise of Evans in “bragger.”

Ballerini and Evans have also worked on music together. In 2018, the year after their wedding, Ballerini joined Evans on his song “Dance With Me.” Since their split, Evans has released a song inspired by their break up.

3 Kelsea Ballerini And Morgan Evans Are Divorced

Unfortunately, Kelsea Ballerini and Morgan Evans’s happiness was not meant to last. On August 29th, 2022, Ballerini shared on social media that she had filed for divorce from Evans. Ballerini and Evans joined the other celebrities that shockingly got divorced in 2022. Evans has since hinted that the decision was not mutual.

Ballerini and Evans had been in therapy together for years. They had also previously separated privately for a short amount of time. Ballerini asked her fans for privacy during the difficult time, but made it clear she was eager to dive into music to process the divorce.

2 Kelsea Ballerini Cried In The Bathtub

One of the reasons why country music fans love Kelsea Ballerini so much is due to her transparency. In her music, Ballerini is very blunt about her experiences and feelings, which listeners can relate to. That transparency that fans love so much has not wavered, as Ballerini has been candid with fans about the hard time she is going through.

Ballerini shared a clip of her in her bathtub in early September on her TikTok set to Katie Gregson-MacLeod’s “Complex.” Her mascara is smeared, making it clear to fans that she had been crying. Ballerini has never been one to hide her pain, and fans love her strength.

1 Kelsea Ballerini Is Focused On Healing

Going through a divorce is traumatic for anyone, but it is particularly tough when in the public eye. Privacy is hard to come by as a celebrity, making hard and complex times even harder. Kelsea Ballerini, however, does not seem concerned with the public’s opinion of her divorce.

Ballerini just released her most recent album Subject To Change and is gearing up for her tour. The singer has fully thrown herself into her music and personal growth. While still letting herself grieve the loss of her marriage, Ballerini is on a mental health journey of healing.
